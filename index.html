    BrahaBraha is a mind game that focuses on memory and pattern recognition. It is presented by a grid of cards, each with a unique symbol or image. The primary objective of the game entails flipping over cards in order to match its respective pairs, thus, the players have to employ their attention and be mentally focused so they can easily keep track of location where similar cards may be hidden. The idea, therefore, is to offer players an enjoyable yet challenging experience all while exercising cognitive abilities such as memory retention and pattern recognition.

    Glutton: The Feeding Man Game

    Glutton is an engaging and entertaining mobile and web game where players must feed a gluttonous man within a specified time limit. The game challenges players to quickly identify and provide the man's preferred food while avoiding inedible objects. If players accidentally feed the man non-food items or fail to feed him an adequate amount, his health decreases, and he becomes grumpy. The ultimate goal is to keep the man well-fed and happy until the end of the game.
